I'm a researcher, designer, and educator pursuing a Master's degree in Design for Interaction at TU Delft in The Netherlands. I'm interested in how humans and autonomous agents (broadly robots, smart products, and AI-powered entities) collaborate. Specifically, I'm interested in how agents communicate the reasoning of their decisions and actions, and how they express their uncertainty. Before moving to the Netherlands, I taught courses in data visualization, web design, and data journalism at the University of Miami. In 2018, I investigated hate crimes in America as a Carnegie-Knight News21 Fellow. I previously studied Photography at the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications, and Mechanical Engineering at MIT.
