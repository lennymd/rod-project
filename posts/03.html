A thank you note

I got this in the mail from one of my students who recently graduated. I've been interested in teaching and mentoring students since Fall 2015 and it's been some of the most fun I've had so far. I love the feeling when things click with a student and they level up their work or find a new direction to work with.

This note arrived during a really rough week this semester when I was doubting myself and what I wanted to do, but it reminded me of how much the work I do can affect other people. I want to improve myself as a designer and researcher and critic so I can better help and inspire others.

I made this site to collect the different facets of my practice in one place. Not every post is long, but each post speaks to the kind of practicioner I am and the things I value. It's made by hand and there may be dead links. If you find one, let me know. All my contact info is on my website.
